Travel


Car Pool
Reach out to other friends and family of Nathan and Jen and organise a carpool.
Train and Taxi
Take a train from Newcastle Interchange to Branxton Station, then take a taxi to Pokolbin. This journey typically takes around 1 hour and 7 minutes
Drive
You can drive from Newcastle to Pokolbin. The distance is approximately 65 km and the journey usually takes around 50 minutes
Bus
Rover Coaches operates a bus from Newcastle Interchange, Stand A to Vincent St At Edward St every 4 hours.
Train and Bus
Take the train from Newcastle Interchange to Maitland Station, then take the line 164 bus from Maitland Station, Railway St to Vincent St At Edward St1
